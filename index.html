How to play

Word detective field kit

Practice cards

  1. Tap a card to flip it and reveal the meaning and the line from the story.
  2. Tap "say it" to hear the word read aloud.
  3. Use the page dropdown to focus on one page at a time.
  4. Tap "shuffle cards" to mix up the order, or "flip all cards" to see every meaning at once.

Dig site challenge

  1. Tap "read it aloud" and say the word clearly.
  2. Read it correctly and the tile cracks open and disappears — that word is excavated!
  3. Not quite right? Try again.
  4. Watch the progress bar fill up until the whole site is excavated.
  5. Tap "shuffle cards" for a new order, or "reset dig site" to play again from the start.
